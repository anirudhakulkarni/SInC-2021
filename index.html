About Us

The Student Incubation Cell, IIT Delhi is formed to support budding Entrepreneurs who can make a mark on the world map through their next generation technology by leading from the front. As they say “It’s not about ideas, it’s about making ideas happen and we are committed to pave the way with every step they take.

Our VISION
